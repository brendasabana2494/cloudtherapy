"Let's Talk About It"

Mental health has long been left on the back burner of important conversations in Kenya. The lack of discussion in no way makes the problem disappear. Instead, it encourages the growth of stigma, it allows shame to fester and contributes to the large number of people who are suffering in silence.

Cloud therapy seeks to the break the mould. This is a platform by the youth for the youth whose objective is to drive the conversation. Our initiative 'Let's talk about it' aims to break the stigma and create a sense of normalcy around mental health, by encouraging active and interactive conversations around the topic that has long since been dubbed as "taboo" in our country.

Our platform offers educative information, psychiatric help and a safe space with a supportive uplifting community. Join us and be a part of the change.

Who We Are

We are a group of young people under eMentoring Africa (create hyperlink' to eMA website) passionate about mental health and seeking to bring awareness to mental wellness. Cloud Therapy is our way of breaking the stigma and create a sense of normalcy around mental health under the initiative 'Let's Talk About It'.

Our Story

Every year, thousands of people, especially the youth, fall victim to mental illnesses, because they are unable to open up to the people who are around them; people who are supposed to help them. As a result they bottle up what they are suffering from, until it’s too late and there is yet another case of a successful suicide attempt.

This is why Cloud Therapy was conceived. We decided that the way we can try to curb this problem is to provide an opportunity for people, especially the youth, to share their problem anonymously without being judged. It is easier to talk to strangers about mental health problems than it is to open up to someone we know as we fear judgement from them. No judgement means the youth are able to share their issues with others and prevent mental illnesses. In turn that means less people end up depressed or suicidal.
